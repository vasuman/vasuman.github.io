Ator

Ator is a game in which the player controls the motion of the character by tilting the device. As the device tilts, the perspective of the camera also tilts giving the illusion of a fixed surface. The player taps on the screen to fire in that direction.

The ground texture is supposed to give you a techno-feel. Most 3D models in this game are platonic solids or shapes that can be rigged in less than 3 steps in Blender.

I made this game using the libGDX game engine.
